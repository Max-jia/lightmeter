## 3. 详细描述

Tired of spending nights emailing clients instead of shooting?

Lightmeter is an AI-native CRM built specifically for photographers. It connects to your Gmail, reads incoming inquiries, and drafts professional replies while you're behind the lens.

What makes Lightmeter different from a generic CRM:

🤖 **AI Inbox** — Gmail integration. AI classifies inquiries (new lead, spam, client reply) and drafts responses for you. Just review and send.

🔗 **One-Link Proposals** — Combine proposal, contract, and payment into a single link. Client opens it, signs the contract, and pays with a card — all in one flow.

✍️ **E-Signature Built-in** — No DocuSign subscription needed. Client types their name to sign the contract, which is legally valid under the ESIGN Act.

💰 **Stripe Payments** — Accept credit card payments directly. Stripe Connect support for photographers who want payouts to their own Stripe account.

📊 **Dashboard & Analytics** — See monthly revenue, active clients, drafts ready, and upcoming shoots at a glance.

📅 **Calendar** — Track shoot dates, payment deadlines, and client milestones.

---

**Why I built this:** I'm a photographer who got tired of juggling Gmail, Google Docs, Stripe, and a calendar to manage clients. Lightmeter puts everything in one place and lets AI do the busywork.

**Tech stack:** Next.js, Supabase, DeepSeek AI, Stripe, Gmail API.

**Try it free for 14 days.** No credit card required. Cancel anytime.
